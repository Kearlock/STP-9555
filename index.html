Welcome to the Realm

Step into a storybook kingdom locked in time and ice

Enter Enchanted Kingdom: Frost Curse, where an age-old battle between light and darkness has reached a chilling climax. As the rightful heir, you're thrust into a kingdom teetering on the edge of ruin—its magic faltering, its people silenced, its crown abandoned. The realm is rich with ancient history, elemental forces, and mysterious alliances.

From opulent throne rooms to frostbitten glades, every corner holds secrets. You won’t just observe this story—you’ll shape it through clever decisions and determined exploration. Whether you're a hidden object veteran or a newcomer to the genre, prepare to lose yourself in a realm where every snowflake carries a story.

Storyline

The frost came without warning. The betrayal was no accident

After the king's sudden death, you—his only living descendant—must face your bloodline’s darkest truth: a banished sorceress has returned, wielding a frost curse born of forgotten magic. Her plan is clear—freeze the kingdom, control its magic, and rewrite its history with her at the helm

But there’s still hope. Guided by your father’s last journal and the memories of a once-proud empire, you must reclaim the relics of the royal bloodline and ignite dormant magical sanctuaries. Along your path, you’ll forge bonds with spellcasters, battle cursed beasts, and make impossible decisions

Every step forward unravels more of the truth—and brings you closer to a final confrontation that will define the kingdom’s future

Gameplay

An icy world of secrets—waiting to be seen

In Enchanted Kingdom, your wits are your strongest weapon. Each chapter introduces beautifully illustrated scenes teeming with hidden objects, enchanted mechanisms, and logic-based conundrums

Seek out items tucked beneath snowdrifts, decode cryptic runes, and assemble relics that unlock ancient passageways. Puzzles vary in complexity and style—from elemental matching games to rotating magic rings that power enchanted gates

Hidden object scenes are dynamic, with some items revealed only after triggering special actions. The hint system is robust yet optional—great for players who enjoy a challenge or those who want a helping hand.

Bonus Chapter

The frost cursed the land—but a darker truth haunts its shadows

Once the main story concludes, an epic bonus chapter awaits. The Knight’s Legacy follows a mysterious armored figure rumored to have defied both king and curse. Track his footsteps through forbidden catacombs, glacial caverns, and battle-scarred temples.

This chapter weaves a tale of redemption, betrayal, and ancient vows—all while adding new puzzles and characters to your journey. Expect deeper emotional stakes, dual-perspective storytelling, and a climactic final twist that reframes everything you thought you knew.

Collectibles

Artifacts of beauty and power lie just beneath the frost

Every region you visit contains rare and enchanted items waiting to be uncovered. From phoenix-feather scrolls to moonlight pearls, each collectible adds to your growing library of magical curiosities

Earn aquarium creatures to display in your enchanted sanctuary, and unlock hidden story scrolls that reveal ancient kingdom lore. These items also grant visual upgrades, custom backgrounds, and music tracks

Some can only be found through sharp observation or solving side puzzles—rewarding your exploration with deeper immersion

Replayability

Return again—and uncover what you missed

Once completed, scenes remain open for replay, allowing you to refine your skills and discover missed secrets.

With over 40 achievements—including rare and hidden challenges—you’re always just a step away from a new goal.

Compete against your own records or try themed replays (e.g., no hints, time limits, or item combos).

Each achievement unlocks badges, profile effects, and special lore entries.

Whether you’re a speed-runner, lore-hunter, or perfectionist, Enchanted Kingdom offers a rewarding reason to return.

Design

Where every pixel shivers with enchantment

Crafted by a dedicated team of artists and composers, the game’s visual style combines fairytale charm with gothic elegance

Frozen castles glow with ethereal blues, while cursed woods swirl with animated mist and flickering spirits

Characters are fully voiced, animated, and uniquely stylized, adding personality to each encounter

The soundtrack is dynamic—swelling in moments of triumph and whispering through corridors of sorrow

Ambient sound effects, like creaking trees and echoing footsteps, help anchor you in the world

Play with headphones to feel every chill and crescendo

Hints

Play your way—with or without help

While the game is entirely free, optional support tools are available to smooth your journey. Use them as little or as often as you wish

Hints reveal object locations, while skips let you bypass mini-games you’re stuck on. These tools are balanced for fairness—they never trivialize gameplay, but gently nudge you forward

For expert players, completing chapters without any assistance unlocks elite titles, profile effects, and permanent in-game bonuses

The choice is yours: embrace the full challenge or enjoy the story at your own pace

Offline Mode

No signal? No worries. Your kingdom awaits

Once the game is downloaded, you can play it entirely offline—no internet required. Whether you're traveling, commuting, or simply relaxing at home, your progress is always saved locally.

Every puzzle, scene, and hidden object is available in offline mode, including the bonus chapter and side quests. When you reconnect, achievements and collectibles automatically sync to your profile.
