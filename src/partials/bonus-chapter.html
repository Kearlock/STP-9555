Bonus Chapter

The frost cursed the land—but a darker truth haunts its shadows

Once the main story concludes, an epic bonus chapter awaits. The Knight’s Legacy follows a mysterious armored figure rumored to have defied both king and curse. Track his footsteps through forbidden catacombs, glacial caverns, and battle-scarred temples.

This chapter weaves a tale of redemption, betrayal, and ancient vows—all while adding new puzzles and characters to your journey. Expect deeper emotional stakes, dual-perspective storytelling, and a climactic final twist that reframes everything you thought you knew.
